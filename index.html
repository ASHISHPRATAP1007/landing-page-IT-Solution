Our Quality Services

Loan Management

Help synergize loan documentation process, loan sanction process, installments, securitization of debt, disbursement process, product details, notifies clients with payment reminders, provides debit notes, and loan recovery process in case of default.

Contact Us

Client Management

Enables instant availability of client details for ready reference, including client address, contact details, client lead, bank account details, loan amount, loan type, credit score, financial history, employment status, and collateral information.

Contact Us

Approvals

Facilitates the entire loan processing cycle with a robust interface, from loan application to disbursement, matching clients. Also enables handling multiple approvals, sending notifications for loan approvals, and tracking repayment schedules.

Contact Us

Payments

Integrates payments via various methods such as Electronic Fund Transfer (EFT), credit and debit card processing, top-up recurring payments, along with sending automated SMS and email alerts to clients, ensuring timely reminders and seamless transactions.

Contact Us

Accounting

Showcases a powerful accounting suite that includes account configuration, accounts receivable and payable, bank reconciliation and rescheduling, invoicing, income-expense analysis, liquidity analysis, and profitability analysis.

Contact Us

Reports

Provides detailed reporting and accurate analysis with regards to payments, loan, credit scores, opening-closing balance, profit-loss, and balance sheets. Also, clients need base reports like annual payments of interest and principal, repayment schedules, etc.
